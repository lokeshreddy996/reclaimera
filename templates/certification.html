Sustainability Made Simple for Your Community & Business

Managing bulky textile and household waste—like old clothes, curtains, and uniforms—is a massive headache for property managers and businesses.

Reclaim Era offers a seamless, zero-cost collection service that not only clears your space but officially certifies your commitment to the environment. By partnering with us, you aren't just disposing of waste; you are actively contributing to Bengaluru's circular economy and funding the education of underprivileged children.

Your Path to Certification in 3 Simple Steps

We handle the logistics so you can focus on the impact.

📋 Step 1: The Free Assessment

Our operations team visits your site to understand your waste generation patterns. We work with your facility managers to identify a strategic, convenient spot for collection.

🚚 Step 2: The Collection Drive

The Weekend Drive: We organize a high-energy "Revive & Rise" collection event for your residents or employees.

The Permanent Hub: We install a beautifully designed, permanent Reclaim Era collection bin directly on your premises for ongoing donations.

🏆 Step 3: Certification & Reporting

Every kilogram collected is meticulously weighed and tracked at our Kogilu hub. Your organization receives an official "Zero Waste Green Certificate" detailing your exact environmental impact (e.g., "500 kgs diverted from landfills").
