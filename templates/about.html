The Reclaim Era Journey

Reclaim Era is the environmental sustainability initiative of Lamp Educational and Charitable Trust (LEACT). Founded on March 26, 2019, by Jaganathan Rajagopal in R.K. Hegde Nagar, our mission began with a simple but urgent dual purpose: to protect our city by keeping textile and household waste out of landfills and open fires, and to secure a brighter future for underprivileged children.

While LEACT focuses on providing quality education and infrastructure to children who need it most, Reclaim Era works to fund that mission through a circular economy. By collecting, sorting, and upcycling waste at our Kogilu hub, we transform discarded items into the funds that pay our teachers and build our classrooms.

From a humble beginning to a growing movement, we transform discarded materials into resources that fund education, empower communities, and protect our environment.

The "Waste to Wisdom" Philosophy

At Reclaim Era, we believe that "waste" is simply a valuable resource in the wrong place. We refuse to accept the "use-and-throw" culture that clogs our landfills and poisons our air.

🌍 Our Vision

To build a sustainable world where everyday waste is transformed into wisdom—giving discarded items a second life to support those in need, while empowering the next generation with the education to protect our environment.

🎯 Our Mission

To transform Bengaluru's everyday waste into wisdom by reclaiming textiles and household items to fund education, create green livelihoods, and protect our environment.
