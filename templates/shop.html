Our products are not made in a massive, faceless factory. They are carefully designed and stitched by the women of our Upcycling Academy. By transforming waste management into a skilled livelihood, we have empowered our artisans to earn a stable, dignified income, creating true micro-entrepreneurship right here in North Bengaluru.
